About us

At BLATTÉ, it's all about mindful moments. We offer you a place where you can find peace in the midst of a busy life. Discover new flavors, get inspired, and enjoy the time – your time.

Our values

We stand for uncompromising quality. Our roots are in Europe, but our vision extends across the globe. It's not about tradition; it's about the here and now. It's about what truly matters. Our products are thoughtfully crafted, and our design is clear and functional. We believe in the essentials – experiences that bring joy and stay with you. We represent diversity and premium products that range from tea and accessories to tea-infused chocolate, paper, and gifts. Sustainability is more than a trend to us; it's our promise to you and to the world we live in.

Our culture

We are more than just tea. For us, it's about people, community, and the feeling of being welcome. Our team lives and breathes what we do with passion and dedication. We create spaces and moments that inspire and connect.

Our ambition

We are setting new standards in everything we do, from selecting our partners to the moment you hold your PAPER & TEA product in your hands. We are demanding – of ourselves and of what we offer you. And we are on our way to becoming Europe's leading premium tea brand. Step by step.
