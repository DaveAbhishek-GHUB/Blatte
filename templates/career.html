About us

At BLATTÉ, we offer more than just products—we create a space to experience community, share ideas, and discover the beauty of life. Our high-quality, thoughtfully crafted offerings, from tea and accessories to gifts, are designed to foster positive experiences and inspiring moments. With clear, functional design and a strong commitment to sustainability, we set new standards. Our team passionately embodies our values, ensuring that every interaction with us conveys a sense of warmth and enjoyment. We aim to become Europe's leading premium tea brand while making a sustainable impact on the world.

At BLATTÉ, we are more than just tea—we are about people, community, and creating a welcoming atmosphere. Our passionate and dedicated team crafts inspiring and connecting moments. We set new standards in everything we do, from selecting our partners to the moment you hold a BLATTÉ product. Our ambition is to become Europe's leading premium tea brand, step by step.
